Feu Follet

Çağan Korkmaz, Zihan Liu, Kettu Kautto, Shurong Han, Kathleen "Kai" Lindgren, Boris

A small sokoban-style puzzle game I worked on for a class with a group of 5 people. This project was my first experience with a group of this size, and a great experience in task management. I helped with general development in unity and later on with the art, we didn't have a artist on the team, so I had to learn the 3D art pipeline. I also went in more depth with the Unity animation system which was a good experience, since I find it really annoying to deal with.

I still use Blender for my personal Projects and I quite enjoy doing so.
